The Scientist Behind the Stone

Jonathan Ghikas

Jonathan Ghikas — licensed wildlife conservationist, safari guide, and founder of Nexlora Enterprise Ltd. Born and raised in Morogoro, Tanzania. With a BSc in Wildlife Science & Conservation from the University of Dar es Salaam and years guiding in Mikumi National Park, he brings deep ecological and scientific understanding to ethical gemstone sourcing.

Luminara Gems is a premier division of Nexlora Enterprise Ltd, a unified ecosystem dedicated to showcasing the brilliance and ecological heritage of Tanzania to the world.
